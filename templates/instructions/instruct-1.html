Instructions

In this experiment, you have to look for treasure hidden under tiles. On every round there will be 20 tiles and the treasure will be hidden beneath one of them.

At the start of each round you may be able to check under some of the tiles. For instance, you may be allowed to check under five tiles.

Once you are done checking you have to decide where to go for the treasure. If you managed to find the treasure during the checking period then this is easy, however, if you did not find it you will have to guess where to go.
