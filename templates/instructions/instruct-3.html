Instructions

To help you make your decision, you will be shown the decisions of the previous group of MTurk workers and how well those players did as a result. For example, when deciding whether or not to hire an accountant you may be told that:

In the previous group of players, 24 members hired an accountant, this cost them 16 points each.

However, 16 members did not hire an accountant. They were fined 20 points each.

These are the decisions of past participants and they won't necessarily reflect the decisions of the people you are playing with. However, all your fellow players will be shown the same information as you.
